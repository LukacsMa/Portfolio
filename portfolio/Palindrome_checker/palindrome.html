Palindrome checker

A palindrome is a word, number, phrase, or another sequence of characters which reads the same backward as forward, such as "taco cat" or "madam" or "racecar" or the number "10801". Sentence-length palindromes may be written when allowances are made for adjustments to capital letters, punctuation, and word dividers, such as "A man, a plan, a canal, Panama!", "Was it a car or a cat I saw?" or "No 'x' in Nixon".
